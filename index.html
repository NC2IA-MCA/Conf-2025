About the Conference


The National Conference on Computational Intelligence and Applications (NC2IA) brings together researchers, practitioners and academicians to discuss advancements, challenges, and future directions in the field of computational intelligence. This event aims to foster collaboration and knowledge sharing among professionals from various domains of artificial intelligence and machine learning. We believe that this event will provide an opportunity to learn from one another, exchange ideas, and explore innovative ways to shape the future of AI. In addition to oral presentations and an innovation exhibition, this event will provide an opportunity for researchers and students to showcase their innovative products.

The primary objectives of a national conference on Computational Intelligence and Applications revolve around knowledge exchange, research advancement, networking, bridging theory and practice, industry relevance, education, and publication opportunities. These objectives collectively aim to foster innovation, collaboration, and progress in the field of computational intelligence.
